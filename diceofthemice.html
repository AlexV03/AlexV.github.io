Dice of the Mice

Project Duration: 48 hours
Role: Game Designer & Programmer
Team Size: 4
Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Date: June 2022

About

Dice of the mice was made during the GMTK Game jam in 2022. The theme of the jam was Dice. We made a turn based action game, you throw the dice and with that you can move or attack. When its the enemies turn they can also throw a dice and move or attack.

My Contribution

I have been doing the programming for the game. It was a nice and chaotic experience creating the turn based system and the enemy behavior. I hadnt done a turn based system before and little with enemy ai behior but it was fun to try out some new stuff and i am happy with the results.
