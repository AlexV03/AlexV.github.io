Fatal façade

Project Duration: 48 hours
Role: Programmer
Team Size: 15
Date: February 2026

My Thoughts

Working with this team was great. Everyone had many ideas of what we could make, and eventually we came up with the idea to make the environment in a masquerade ball. We had a nice variety of people to make this game, and everyone was also helpful with each other. If you needed help, there was almost always someone who could help. Working with this team was really fun, and to also get to know them—it is always nice to know new people where you can interact with and exchange new information about your discipline. I learned many new things with this game jam and people, and it was a great experience to witness.
